Major cross-border issues are forcing EU to demonstrate its added value. What to do about European defence, now that United States is withdrawing? How do we stop the deterioration of natural environment? Other parts of the world are investing heavily in industry politics; how will Europe respond? Migration continues to pose a headache. The official European story is that such developments require greater powers for EU, higher EU budget, Eurobonds and a pan-European tax system. According to traditional federalists, it is time to acknowledge that only a transfer of sovereignty can put an end to fragmentation between EU member states.
