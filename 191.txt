European citizens need their institutions to recognize the demands of interdependence and global risk. They need global vision — not just local action. 
David Miliband is the president and CEO of the International Rescue Committee.
Any political consultant will tell you that all politics is local. However, this year’s European election will test that proposition.
There’s plenty of local dissent against governing parties, but from Gaza to Ukraine and migration to food and energy prices, it’s a luxury to believe that the solutions to these challenges are local. Foreign policy is no longer simply a nice-to-have — it has become a need-to-have.
