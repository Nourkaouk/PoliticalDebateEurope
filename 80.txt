The Council of EU can’t use the fact there were ongoing international negotiations on an issue as an argument to refuse to provide opinion of its legal services on the matter to public, EU’s top court said Wednesday in a win for legal charity ClientEarth.
More than 20 labor unions and NGOs called today for a “comprehensive ban” of Amazon, following the Parliament’s rare move to deactivate the e-commerce giant’s 14 lobbyist badges. They argue that Amazon reps should be barred from entering the Parliament as guests and that MEPs should refuse to meet with the company at other locations.
